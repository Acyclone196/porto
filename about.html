About

John

Wildlife · Street · Travel Photographer

I'm John — a passionate photographer with a love for wildlife, street life, and travel. Camera in hand, I've wandered through the savannas of Uganda and Namibia, the vibrant streets of India and Bangkok, and the wild landscapes of Brazil.

Photography is my way of slowing down and paying attention — noticing the light, the moment, the story that's always already there. Whether I'm tracking animals at dawn or drifting through a busy market, I'm looking for that one frame that says it all.

This site is a personal collection of images I love. I hope they take you somewhere.
