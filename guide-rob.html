Driver Guide · Uganda

Rob John

Mabamba · Murchison · Kibale · Bwindi · The Nile

Chimpanzee TrekkingGorilla TrekkingKibale ForestBwindiUganda

There are guides who find animals, and there are guides who find everything else — the road that doesn't flood, the ranger who will give you extra time, the lodge that has rooms left. Rob John is the second kind.

Rob drove us across Uganda for more than two weeks — from Mabamba Swamp to Murchison Falls, south-west to Kibale for the chimpanzees, through Queen Elizabeth, into Bwindi for the gorillas, down into Kyambura Gorge, and finally east to the Nile. He drove all of it with the easy competence of someone who has been doing it for years.

At Kibale he introduced us to the ranger and spoke quietly in Luganda. Then: "He knows where the family went last night. We'll find them early." We did.

Uganda is a complicated country to move through quickly — the roads unpredictable, the logistics of permit timings difficult to coordinate. Rob coordinated all of it and made it feel effortless.

Photographer's NoteRob John is not a wildlife guide in the traditional sense — he is a driver and logistics expert who happens to know Uganda better than almost anyone. Without him, the Uganda trip would have been possible but considerably less.
