Safari Guide · Mashatu Tent Camp · Northern Tuli · Botswana

Dan & Morris

LocationMashatu Tent Camp
Year2023
TrackerMorris
Big CatsNorthern TuliMashatu Reserve

There are guides who find animals and guides who find the right animals at the right moment. Dan is the second kind. He guides at Mashatu Tent Camp with the particular calm authority of someone who knows their ground not just professionally but personally.

I met Dan in 2023, on the trip I will always call the iPhone year. I had decided to leave my Sony at home. Dan, with his tracker Morris, proceeded to deliver some of the finest leopard sightings I have had anywhere on the continent.

What I remember most about Dan is his steadiness. He does not get excited in the way that is performative. His excitement, when it comes, is quiet and genuine. He communicates with Morris the same way — small signals, minimal words, a shared language built over many drives.

The Northern Tuli Block in 2023 was, for the leopards at least, an extraordinary year. On a trip I had deliberately designed to be camera-free, Dan gave me more to photograph than almost any trip I can remember.

Photographer's NoteThe image on my iPhone of two leopards in a tree with an elephant herd moving beneath them is one of my favourite images from that year. Not because of what the phone could do. Because of where Dan put us.
